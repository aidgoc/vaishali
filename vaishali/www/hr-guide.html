1. Overview

DSPL has 179 employees across Service, Sales, Production, R&D, Marketing, and Management departments. The HR module provides self-service access to core HR functions directly from the PWA — no need to log into the ERPNext desk.

  • Leave: Check balance, apply for leave, track approval status
  • Expenses: File expense claims with multiple line items, track reimbursement
  • Advances: Request salary advances, track repayment status
  • Salary slips: View salary history with earnings and deductions breakdown
  • Attendance: GPS-based check-in and check-out with live timer
  • Budget tracking: Monitor personal and team expense budgets
Access the PWA at dgoc.logstop.com/field and navigate to HR Hub. All notifications are sent instantly via Telegram.

3. Leave management

Check your leave balance

Who: All employees
  1. Go to #/leave in the PWA
  2. At the top, you will see balance cards for each leave type — these show your remaining days from your Leave Allocation
Leave typeDescription
Casual LeaveShort personal leave, planned or unplanned
Compensatory OffEarned by working on holidays or weekends
Leave Without PayWhen all paid leave is exhausted
Privilege LeaveEarned leave, planned in advance
Sick LeaveMedical leave with supporting documentation

Apply for leave

Who: All employees  |  Where: #/leave → New
  1. Tap "New Leave" or the + button
  2. Select the leave type from the dropdown (Casual Leave, Compensatory Off, Leave Without Pay, Privilege Leave, Sick Leave)
  3. Set the From Date and To Date
  4. If you need only a half day, toggle Half Day on and select which date is the half day
  5. Enter a reason for the leave
  6. Tap Submit

After submission, the leave application status is set to "Open" (pending approval).

What happens after you apply

  1. Your department leave approvers receive a Telegram notification with your name, leave type, dates, and reason
  2. If no department approvers are configured, the notification falls back to default managers (harsh@dgoc.in, bng@dgoc.in)
  3. When the approver approves or rejects your leave, you receive a Telegram notification with the decision

Check leave status

Who: All employees  |  Where: #/leave

Your leave list shows status pills next to each application:

Pill colourStatusMeaning
GreenApprovedLeave is approved — you are good to go
RedRejectedLeave was rejected — check with your approver
YellowOpenPending approval — waiting for approver action
Leave balance is checked at the time of submission. If you do not have enough balance for the selected leave type, the system will show an error. Switch to Leave Without Pay if all paid leave is exhausted.

4. Expense claims

File an expense claim

Who: All employees  |  Where: #/expenses → New Claim
  1. Go to #/expenses and tap "New Claim"
  2. Add expense lines — for each line, fill in:
FieldWhat to enterExample
Expense TypeSelect from: Travel Fare, Food, Calls, Medical, Accommodation, Other TravelTravel Fare
DateDate the expense was incurred2026-04-10
AmountAmount in INR1,500
DescriptionBrief description of the expenseCab to Reliance site, Jamnagar
  1. You can add multiple expense lines — a running total is shown at the bottom
  2. Tap Submit when all lines are entered

What happens after you submit

  1. Managers receive a Telegram notification: "New expense claim from {name}: ₹{amount}"
  2. When the claim is approved or rejected, you receive a Telegram notification with the decision

Budget cap system

Soft enforcement — claims are never blocked

Each employee has a monthly expense budget cap. The system enforces this with warnings only — it never blocks a claim submission.

LevelCap
Senior roles₹25,000 per month
Other employees₹15,000 per month
EPS vertical (team level)₹2,50,000 per month

What happens at budget thresholds

ThresholdAction
At 80% usageA warning comment is automatically added to the expense claim
At 100% usageTelegram alert sent to your direct manager (reports_to) or default managers (harsh@dgoc.in, bng@dgoc.in)
Claims are never blocked by the budget system. The cap is advisory — the system warns but always allows submission. Managers decide whether to approve.
Check your budget status anytime at #/budget — it shows a progress bar of your spent amount versus your monthly cap.

6. Salary slips

View your salary history

Who: All employees  |  Where: #/salary
  1. Go to #/salary in the PWA
  2. You will see a list of your salary slips showing: month, year, and net pay
  3. Only submitted (finalised) salary slips are visible — drafts are not shown
  4. The 20 most recent salary slips are displayed

Salary slip detail view

Tap any salary slip to see the full breakdown:

SectionWhat it shows
Gross PayTotal earnings before deductions
EarningsLine items for each earning component (Basic, HRA, Conveyance, etc.)
DeductionsLine items for each deduction (PF, ESI, Professional Tax, TDS, etc.)
Net PayFinal take-home amount after all deductions
Salary slips appear here only after the accounts team has processed and submitted payroll for that month. If a month is missing, check with HR.

7. Attendance (GPS check-in/out)

Check in and check out

Who: All employees  |  Where: #/attendance
  1. Go to #/attendance in the PWA
  2. The system will request your GPS location — allow the permission when prompted
  3. Your GPS accuracy is shown as a coloured pill:
    • Green — accuracy ≤ 20 metres (good signal)
    • Orange — accuracy > 20 metres (weak signal, move to an open area)
  4. Tap the green "CHECK IN" button to start your workday
  5. A live timer appears showing HH:MM:SS since check-in
  6. When your workday ends, tap the blue "CHECK OUT" button

Additional details

  • GPS coordinates are recorded and shown on screen — you can expand them to see the exact location
  • Duplicate prevention: The system uses idempotency keys to prevent accidental double check-ins (e.g., if you tap the button twice quickly)
  • Both check-in and check-out times are recorded in ERPNext as Employee Checkin records
Make sure your phone's GPS is enabled and you have a clear view of the sky for best accuracy. Indoor locations may show orange (low accuracy) — step outside briefly if needed.

10. For managers

Leave approval

Who: Department leave approvers, HR managers
  1. Receive a Telegram notification when an employee applies for leave
  2. Log in to Frappe desk at dgoc.logstop.com/app
  3. Go to Leave Application list or click the link in the notification
  4. Open the leave application
  5. Review the details: leave type, dates, reason, employee's leave balance
  6. Click Approve or Reject
  7. The employee is notified via Telegram automatically

Expense claim approval

Who: Expense approvers, HR managers
  1. Receive a Telegram notification when an employee submits an expense claim
  2. Log in to Frappe desk at dgoc.logstop.com/app
  3. Go to Expense Claim list
  4. Open the claim and review each expense line (type, date, amount, description)
  5. Click Approve or Reject
  6. The employee is notified via Telegram automatically

Budget monitoring

Who: Managers and admins
  • Check the Budget Dashboard at #/budget in the PWA for a team-level or company-wide view
  • You will receive automatic Telegram alerts when any employee in your team exceeds 100% of their monthly budget cap
  • Review budget trends monthly and adjust caps if needed via the Expense Budget DocType in ERPNext desk

Leave approver configuration

Leave approvers are set at the department level in ERPNext:

  1. Go to HR > Department in the desk
  2. Open the relevant department
  3. In the Leave Approvers section, add the employees who should approve leaves for that department
  4. If no approvers are set, leave notifications fall back to default managers

## Headlines

1. **The order-to-cash cycle is not on the system.** 1,017 draft Sales Invoices, 0 submitted. 267 draft Payment Entries, 0 submitted. 0 Purchase Orders, 0 Purchase Invoices. Finance and Procurement are running on shadow tools.
2. **AI agent has one power user and a long tail of nothing.** bng@dgoc.in = 82% of all chat traffic. 7 distinct users in a month against a 179-employee base.
3. **DCR adoption is not a soft-launch problem any more — it's stuck.** 5 employees filed 11 DCRs in 30 days; only 1 of 179 employees has connected Telegram. The PWA push channel doesn't exist for 99.4% of staff.
4. **The HNG email account has thrown 790 connection errors in 30 days** — that's the dominant noise in Error Log and likely silently breaking notifications. Worth investigating today.
5. **80% of queued email is in Error state** (112 of 140). Combine with the IMAP failures and email is partly broken in production despite the recent OAuth integration.
6. **`tabVersion` is 96 MB — 25% of the database.** Ripe for a `delete from tabVersion where creation < ...` pruning job. `tabDeleted Document` at 34 MB is similar.
7. **14 System Managers** out of ~54 users — privilege model is too flat for a 179-person org.
